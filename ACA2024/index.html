Artificial Intelligence and Symbolic Computation
at
Applications of Computer Algebra (ACA) 2024
University of Havana
Havana, Cuba, June 24 - 28, 2024

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Symbolic Computation (SC) are two fields of research devoted to the development of approaches for automating the process of problem solving, in particular mathematical problem solving. The two fields intersect with each other on a large number of underlying problems with symbol objects. A wealth of challenges, ideas, theoretical insights and results, methods and algorithms arise in the interaction of the two fields and research communities. Advanced tools of software technology and system design are needed and a broad spectrum of applications is possible by the combined the problem solving power of the two fields.

This special session of ACA 2024 focuses on Artificial Intelligence & Symbolic Computation, dedicated to exploring the profound interconnection between AI and SC. It aims at shedding light on the intricate relationship between these two fields, showcasing their collaborative potential and innovative applications. Researchers, scholars, and experts in the realms of AI and SC are invited to contribute their talks to the session.

List of Topics

Specific topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Interaction between symbolic computation and machine learning

  • Mathematics-oriented data processing and large language model (LLM) training

  • Translation of mathematical knowledge between various languages

  • Mathematical knowledge management using machine learning

  • Mathematical knowledge representation for machine learning

  • LLM-aided theorem proving

  • Machine learning and computational intelligence

  • Automated reasoning and knowledge discovery

  • Mechanized program verification and debugging

  • Symbolic computation for expert systems and machine learning

  • Computer algebra systems and automated theorem provers

  • Programming languages and systems for symbolic computation
